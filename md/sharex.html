Upload from your desktop using ShareX

First up. What is ShareX?

ShareX is a program used to capture screenshots and upload them to various services. Other files are also supported.

Where can I find ShareX?

You can download ShareX from Their website. It's completely free.

How do I setup ShareX to work with QuadFile?

It's fairly simple.
To make it even simpler. Everything will be linked to screenshots

  • First, open your ShareX window
  • Then, find your destination settings
  • Now, scroll to the bottom until you find the 'custom uploaders' option
  • Download this file
  • Click import -> from file, then navigate to the file you just downloaded
  • You did it!

Wait, it's still not working

You might have forgotten to set it as your default upload destination.

To actually upload a screenshot, press Print Screen to capture your screen

Using Ctrl + Print Screen will also allow you to capture a region of your screen

You can mess with these key bindings and various other settings if you wish
