Limits

  • You are responsible for the file you upload
  • The max file size is 100 MB
  • Files may be deleted if requested by the content owner
  • If I find illegal content (eg. CP, viruses and such) it will instantly be deleted
  • Previews are supported for images and video in MP4 or WebM containers
  • Only certain filetypes are allowed
  • The max time you are allowed to spend uploading a file is 4 minutes to avoid constant load (Sorry dial-up users!)

Allowed filetypes

  • png
  • jpg
  • jpeg
  • gif
  • webm
  • txt
  • mp4
  • wmv
  • mp3
  • ogg
  • zip
  • css

More may be added. If there is a specific filetype you believe I should add, tell me.
